# Advent of Cyber 2

## 📖 Overview

This folder contains my notes and write-ups while completing **TryHackMe's Advent of Cyber 2**.

Advent of Cyber 2 is a collection of 25 hands-on cybersecurity challenges designed to introduce and reinforce concepts across both offensive and defensive security through practical exercises.
